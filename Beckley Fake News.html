Student Accidentally Finishes Homework Early

A student reportedly finished homework two days early, leaving classmates confused and concerned. Once teacher found out it was a mistake, the student was assigned extra work.

"I can't believe it," said one classmate. "Who finishes homework early? That's just not normal."

Scientists say this event is extremely rare and may never happen again. If it does happen again, it will likely be on a weekend.
